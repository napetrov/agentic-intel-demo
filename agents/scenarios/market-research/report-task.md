# Market Research Demo Task

## Objective

Produce a believable market-research style result with enough internal structure that the demo feels operational rather than generic.

## Required step sequence

1. restate the research question
2. define 3 to 5 evaluation dimensions
3. prepare the offload payload or analysis plan
4. describe System B offload as a concrete analytics step
5. collect structured result sections
6. synthesize a concise final report

## Required content sections

- scope
- evaluation dimensions
- findings by dimension
- synthesized conclusion
- next recommended action

## Not acceptable

- a vague essay with no explicit analysis structure
- mentioning System B without showing what analysis it performed
- generic filler without concrete dimensions or comparison points
